Our Brand

KITE was co-founded by a group tea enthusiasts from China, United States, Russia and many other countries, commited to bring healthy traditional Chinese tea into today's modern lifestyle.

And to produce the finest quality Chinese tea, we've requested help from several China reknown tea specialists to find us the best tea leaves around and had the product specially packed in pyramid tea bag. This unique triangular structure container allows the leaves a 50% increase of space to move around compared to the traditional tea bag.

Now everyone can have a taste of our authentic Chinese tea, anytime, anywhere.

Last but not least, in KITE we always believed in sharing is joy in life.
